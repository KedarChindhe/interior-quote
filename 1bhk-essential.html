1 BHK: Essential Plan – ₹4.8 Lakhs*

Entry level interior package for a standard 1 BHK home in Pune.

Rooms Covered

  • Entrance / Foyer
  • Living room
  • Kitchen
  • Bedroom
  • Dining / common basin area

Entrance

  • Shoe rack / storage unit at entrance

Living Room

  • TV unit with storage and open shelves
  • Sofa back panel / feature wall design

Kitchen

  • Modular base and wall units (both sides, as per site)
  • Drawer units with cutlery / thali / tandem drawers
  • Laminate finish shutters with edge banding
  • Overhead cabinets with frosted glass combination

Dining Area

  • 4 seater dining table or foldable breakfast counter (as per layout)

Bedroom

  • 1 bed (king or queen size) with storage
  • Wardrobe with openable / sliding doors
  • Side tables and simple dressing unit

Specification

  • 18mm plywood / blockboard for all fixed furniture
  • BWR / equivalent waterproof ply in kitchen
  • Outer finish in laminate as per selected shade
  • Soft‑close hinges and basic hardware fittings
  • Standard electrical work related to furniture points
  • Interior wall paint in tractor / equivalent emulsion

Other Work

  • Basic false ceiling in living room (simple design)
  • Concealed LED strip / spot lights where required
