3 BHK: Essential Plan – ₹9.5 Lakhs*

Essential interiors for a standard 3 BHK family home in Pune.

Rooms Covered

  • Entrance / Foyer
  • Living room
  • Kitchen
  • Master bedroom
  • Children bedroom
  • Guest / parents bedroom
  • Dining / common basin area

Entrance

  • Shoe rack / storage unit at entrance

Living Room

  • TV unit with storage and open shelves
  • Sofa back panel / simple feature wall design

Kitchen

  • Modular base and wall units (L / parallel as per site)
  • Drawer units with basic organisers
  • Laminate finish shutters
  • Overhead cabinets with some glass shutters

Dining Area

  • 4–6 seater dining table or breakfast counter

Master Bedroom

  • Storage bed (queen / king)
  • Wardrobe with loft
  • Dressing unit

Children Bedroom

  • Wardrobe with partition
  • Study table / work desk
  • Wall shelves / book storage

Guest / Parents Bedroom

  • Wardrobe with loft
  • Optional study / console as space allows

Specification

  • 18mm plywood / blockboard for fixed furniture
  • BWR / equivalent waterproof ply in kitchen
  • Laminate finishes externally
  • Soft‑close hinges where required
  • Electrical points related to furniture
  • Interior wall paint in tractor / equivalent emulsion

Other Work

  • Basic false ceiling in living room
  • LED spot lights as required

*Pricing is indicative and may vary based on exact measurements, site condition, material brand selection and final design approval.
